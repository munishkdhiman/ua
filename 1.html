Company Summary ✨ AI ENHANCED

Indel Automotives Private Limited (IAPL) was formerly known as Aladin Media Production & Advertising Private Limited. As per its annual filings, the company is engaged in dealers, agents, sub-agents, traders, importers, and exporters of vehicles, parts and components, and accessories of different categories of vehicles. The company was incorporated in 2011 and has its registered office located in Mumbai, Maharashtra.

In FY24, the company reported a severe standalone revenue contraction, posting just ₹0.11 Cr, representing a decline of 98.69% year-on-year. Promoter holding stands solidly at 100% via its holding entity, Indel Corporation Private Limited. The company's UA score of 22/100 reflects critical financial distress, massive leverage (D/E of 49.4x), negative reserves (-₹66.76 Cr), and flagged compliance metrics due to systemic GST and EPF payment delays.
