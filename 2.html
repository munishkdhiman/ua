Company Summary ✨ AI ENHANCED

Knight Riders Sports Private Limited (KRSPL) operates the highly successful Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) franchise of the Indian Premier League (IPL). Since its incorporation in 2008, the entity has monetized sports activities, media rights, and sponsorship deals under the governance of the BCCI.

The company represents a highly profitable unlisted entity in the entertainment and events sector. It enjoys a debt-free capital structure, zero open charges, and stellar profitability margins driven by the lucrative ecosystem of the IPL. The global footprint is being actively expanded via subsidiaries in the USA (Major League Cricket) and the Caribbean (CPL).

In FY25, KRSPL recorded standalone revenues of ₹670.67 Cr and an immense PAT of ₹203.71 Cr. Despite the flawless financial and capital structuring, the company is managing extensive legal overheads—tracking over 50 past and present cases involving taxation disputes, FEMA regulations, and insolvency matters.
