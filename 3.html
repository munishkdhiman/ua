Company Summary

GINI & JONY LIMITED is a Public Limited Indian Non-Government Company, incorporated on November 11, 1994, in Maharashtra. The company operates within the consumer products industry, with a core focus on manufacturing. Its primary business involves the design, production, retail, and sale of a diverse range of products tailored for children. This includes kids' apparel, covering various clothing items, as well as accessories and footwear. The company's operations span the entire value chain from manufacturing to direct sales, indicating a vertically integrated or partially integrated business model. Headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, GINI & JONY LIMITED has been active in the Indian market for several decades, establishing its brand in the competitive children's wear and accessories segment. Its business model likely involves a network of retail stores, possibly a mix of company-owned and franchised outlets, alongside potential online sales channels, to reach its target consumer base across India. The company's long-standing presence suggests a sustained effort in product development and market penetration within the children's fashion sector.

− Weaknesses

  • Presence of Bureau Defaults, indicating past credit payment issues.

💡 Opportunities

  • Economic downturns impacting consumer spending on discretionary items like children's fashion.
